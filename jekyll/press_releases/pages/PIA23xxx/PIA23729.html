# Mars Perseverance Sol 16: Left Navigation Camera (Navcam) * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA23xxx/PIA23729_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA23729.jpg) (1280 x 960) (178.6 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA23729.tif) (1280 x 960) (3.5 MB)

Caption:

NASA's Mars Perseverance rover acquired this image using its onboard left Navigation Camera (Navcam). The camera is located high on the rover's mast and aids in driving. This image was acquired on March 7, 2021 (Sol 16) at the local mean solar time of 15:04:10.

Background Info:

NASA's Jet Propulsion Laboratory, which is managed for NASA by Caltech in Pasadena, California, built and manages operations of the Perseverance rover.

For more about Perseverance: mars.nasa.gov/mars2020/
