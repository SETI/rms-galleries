# Lifting Perseverance * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA23xxx/PIA23984_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA23984.jpg) (6720 x 4480) (4.7 MB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA23984.tif) (6720 x 4480) (84.9 MB)

Caption:

A crane stands at the ready to hoist the payload fairing, or nose cone, containing NASA's Mars 2020 Perseverance rover onto the top of an Atlas V launch vehicle. The image was taken at the Vertical Integration Facility at Space Launch Complex 41 at Cape Canaveral Air Force Station in Florida on July 7, 2020.

Background Info:

For more information about the mission, go to https://mars.nasa.gov/mars2020/ .
