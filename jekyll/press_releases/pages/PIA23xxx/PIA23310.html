# Mariner 1 in JPL's Spacecraft Assembly Facility * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA23xxx/PIA23310_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA23310.jpg) (3832 x 3623) (2.9 MB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA23310.tif) (3832 x 3623) (41.7 MB)

Caption:

JPL's Spacecraft Assembly Facility looks more like a hangar in this photo of two engineers standing with Mariner 1 on May 2, 1962. However, the gowning procedures were far less rigorous than they are today. Mariner 1 was destroyed during its attempted launch to Venus.
