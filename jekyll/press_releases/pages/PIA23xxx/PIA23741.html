# Terra Sabaea Channel * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA23xxx/PIA23741_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA23741.jpg) (1402 x 2641) (231.5 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA23741.tif) (1402 x 2641) (2.5 MB)

Caption:


Context image

Today's VIS image shows an unnamed channel in northern Terra Sabaea. The regional boundary between the highlands of Terra Sabaea and lowlands of Utopia Planitia is heavily dissected by both fluid flow channels and extensive faulting.

Orbit Number: 79922 Latitude: 28.8312 Longitude: 70.6552 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2019-12-21 02:28
