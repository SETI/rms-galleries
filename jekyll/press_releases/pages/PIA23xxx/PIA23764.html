# Perseverance on Mars * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA23xxx/PIA23764_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA23764.jpg) (1920 x 1080) (295.3 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA23764.tif) (1920 x 1080) (4.8 MB)

Caption:

This illustration depicts NASA's Perseverance rover operating on the surface of Mars. Perseverance will land at the Red Planet's Jezero Crater a little after 3:40 p.m. EST (12:40 p.m. PST) on Feb. 18, 2021.

Background Info:

For more information about the mission, go to https://mars.nasa.gov/mars2020/ .
