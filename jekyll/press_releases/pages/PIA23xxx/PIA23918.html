# Unloading Mars Gear * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA23xxx/PIA23918_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA23918.jpg) (6720 x 4480) (4.5 MB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA23918.tif) (6720 x 4480) (85.5 MB)

Caption:

Some of the nearly 5,000 pounds (2,270 kilograms) of Perseverance mission flight hardware, test gear and equipment delivered to Kennedy Space Center on May 11, 2020, is unloaded from a NASA Wallops C-130.
