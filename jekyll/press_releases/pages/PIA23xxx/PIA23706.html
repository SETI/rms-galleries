# Shalbatana Vallis * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA23xxx/PIA23706_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA23706.jpg) (868 x 2725) (133.0 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA23706.tif) (868 x 2725) (1.4 MB)

Caption:


Context image

This VIS image shows a section of Shalbatana Vallis. Shalbatana Vallis is located in Xanthe Terra and is one of many channels that empty into Chryse Planitia.

Orbit Number: 79639 Latitude: 8.5729 Longitude: 317.92 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2019-11-27 19:06
