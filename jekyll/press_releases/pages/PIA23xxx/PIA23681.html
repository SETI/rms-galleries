# Voyager 1 Perspective for Family Portrait * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA23xxx/PIA23681_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA23681.jpg) (4000 x 3264) (336.9 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA23681.tif) (4000 x 3264) (2.5 MB)

Caption:


Figure 1

This simulated view, made using NASA's Eyes on the Solar System app, approximates Voyager 1's perspective when it took its final series of images known as the "Family Portrait of the Solar System," including the "Pale Blue Dot" image. Figure 1 shows the location of each image.
