# Crater Channel * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA23xxx/PIA23387_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA23387.jpg) (705 x 1440) (82.9 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA23387.tif) (705 x 1440) (726.9 kB)

Caption:


Context image

Today's VIS image has a channel dissecting a crater rim, flowing from the interior of a large crater into a smaller crater on the large crater's floor. The two craters are unnamed and located in northern Terra Sabaea.

Orbit Number: 77664 Latitude: 25.6553 Longitude: 56.6781 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2019-06-18 04:02
