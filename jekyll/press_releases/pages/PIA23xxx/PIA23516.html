# Some Differences Between Mars 2020 and Curiosity * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA23xxx/PIA23516_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA23516.jpg) (2500 x 1406) (288.0 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA23516.tif) (2500 x 1406) (2.4 MB)

Caption:

NASA's Mars 2020 rover looks virtually the same as Curiosity, but there are a number of differences. One giveaway to which rover you're looking at is 2020's aft cross-beam, which looks a bit like a shopping cart handle.

Background Info:

JPL is building and will manage operations of the Mars 2020 rover for the NASA Science Mission Directorate at the agency's headquarters in Washington.

For more information about the mission, go to https://mars.nasa.gov/mars2020/ .
