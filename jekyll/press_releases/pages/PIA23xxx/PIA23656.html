# Arabia Terra Channel * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA23xxx/PIA23656_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA23656.jpg) (1421 x 2643) (303.5 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA23656.tif) (1421 x 2643) (2.6 MB)

Caption:


Context image

Today's VIS image is located in northern Arabia Terra. This region is dissected with numerous unnamed channels of all sizes and complexities. The channel in this image is one of many small tributary channels that coalesce into larger channels that eventually empty into the lower elevation northern plains.
