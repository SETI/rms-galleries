# Spirit and Opportunity in High Bay 1 * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA23xxx/PIA23244_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA23244.jpg) (3008 x 1960) (682.8 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA23244.tif) (3008 x 1960) (17.7 MB)

Caption:

High Bay 1 looks much as it does today in this photo of NASA's Spirit and Opportunity Mars rovers being tested on Feb. 10, 2003. Some workers are wearing booties and others wear shoes that were stored in the gowning area and cleaned regularly.
