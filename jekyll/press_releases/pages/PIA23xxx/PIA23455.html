# Sand Dunes * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA23xxx/PIA23455_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA23455.jpg) (698 x 1430) (66.9 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA23455.tif) (698 x 1430) (690.2 kB)

Caption:


Context image

Today's VIS image shows a field of sand dunes in an unnamed crater located in Margaritifer Terra. Most sand dunes on Mars are made of dark basaltic sands.

Orbit Number: 78228 Latitude: 6.60598 Longitude: 346.706 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2019-08-03 14:35
