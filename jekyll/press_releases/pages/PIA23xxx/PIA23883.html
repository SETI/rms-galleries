# High School Junior Names NASA's Mars Helicopter * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA23xxx/PIA23883_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA23883.jpg) (1920 x 1080) (249.6 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA23883.tif) (1920 x 1080) (5.1 MB)

Caption:


Vaneeza Rupani holding a postcard of the Mars Helicopter

Vaneeza Rupani (inset), a junior at Tuscaloosa County High School in Northport, Alabama, came up with the name Ingenuity for NASA's Mars Helicopter (an artist's impression of which is seen here) and the motivation behind it for NASA's " Name the Rover " essay contest.

An additional image shows the full portrait of Rupani.

Background Info:

For more information about the mission, go to https://mars.nasa.gov/technology/helicopter/ .
