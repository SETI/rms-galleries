# Baetis Chaos * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA23xxx/PIA23942_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA23942.jpg) (1357 x 2639) (181.9 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA23942.tif) (1357 x 2639) (2.3 MB)

Caption:


Context image

Today's VIS image shows part of Baetis Chaos. This region of chaos is located north of Juventae Chasma. Juventae Chasma is a huge box canyon near Valles Marineris. Water flowed from Juventae Chasma, through Baetis Chaos and created the northward flowing channel called Maja Valles.
