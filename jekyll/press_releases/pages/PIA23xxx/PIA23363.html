# Bahram Vallis * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA23xxx/PIA23363_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA23363.jpg) (704 x 1438) (77.0 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA23363.tif) (704 x 1438) (720.3 kB)

Caption:


Context image

Bahram Vallis crosses this VIS image. Bharam Vallis drains from the the higher elevations of Lunae Planum into the Chryse Planitia basin. At the base of several of the scalloped channel wall sections deposits of material can be seen.
