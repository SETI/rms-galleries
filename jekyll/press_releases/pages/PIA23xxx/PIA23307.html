# Installing SuperCam on the Mars 2020 Rover * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA23xxx/PIA23307_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA23307.jpg) (6240 x 4165) (3.0 MB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA23307.tif) (6240 x 4165) (59.7 MB)

Caption:

Engineers install the SuperCam instrument on Mars 2020's rover. This image was taken on June 25, 2019, in the Spacecraft Assembly Facility at NASA's Jet Propulsion Laboratory, Pasadena, California.

Background Info:

JPL is building and will manage operations of the Mars 2020 rover for the NASA Science Mission Directorate at the agency's headquarters in Washington.

For more information about the mission, go to https://mars.nasa.gov/mars2020/ .
