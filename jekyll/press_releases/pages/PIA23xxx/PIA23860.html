# Locras Valles * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA23xxx/PIA23860_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA23860.jpg) (1365 x 2631) (305.6 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA23860.tif) (1365 x 2631) (2.6 MB)

Caption:


Context image

Today's VIS shows one of the small channels in Locras Valles. Located in Terra Sabaea, Locras Valles is a group of channels that dissect the plains north of several unnamed craters, the rims of which create the highland where the channels start.
