# Volcanic Plains - False Color * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA23xxx/PIA23051_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA23051.jpg) (623 x 2701) (122.9 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA23051.tif) (623 x 2701) (2.3 MB)

Caption:


Context image

This VIS image shows a small part of the extensive volcanic plains located east of Olympus Mons.

The THEMIS VIS camera contains 5 filters. The data from different filters can be combined in multiple ways to create a false color image. These false color images may reveal subtle variations of the surface not easily identified in a single band image.
