# Composing Mars Helicopter * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA23xxx/PIA23157_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA23157.jpg) (8256 x 5504) (6.7 MB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA23157.tif) (8256 x 5504) (110.3 MB)

Caption:

Members of NASA's Mars Helicopter team attach a thermal film enclosure to the fuselage of the flight model (the actual vehicle going to the Red Planet). The image was taken on Feb. 1, 2019, inside the Space Simulator, a 25-foot-wide (7.62-meter-wide) vacuum chamber at NASA's Jet Propulsion Laboratory in Pasadena, California.

Background Info:

For more information about the mission, go to https://mars.nasa.gov/technology/helicopter/ .
