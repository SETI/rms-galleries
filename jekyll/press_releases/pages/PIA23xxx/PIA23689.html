# Kepler Planet 1649c Artist's Illustration From Space * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA23xxx/PIA23689_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA23689.jpg) (3840 x 2160) (563.2 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA23689.tif) (3840 x 2160) (15.3 MB)

Caption:

This artist's concept shows exoplanet Kepler-1649c orbiting around its host red dwarf star. This exoplanet is in its star's habitable zone (the distance where liquid water could exist on the planet's surface) and is the closest to Earth in size and temperature found yet in Kepler's data.
