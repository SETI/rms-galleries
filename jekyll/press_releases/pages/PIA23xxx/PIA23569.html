# Ophir Chasma Landslide * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA23xxx/PIA23569_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA23569.jpg) (691 x 1426) (74.9 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA23569.tif) (691 x 1426) (691.0 kB)

Caption:


Context image

This VIS image shows part of Ophir Chasma. Ophir Chasma is part of Valles Marineris, the largest canyon system on Mars. At the top of the image is a portion of a large landslide deposit which originate at the northern wall of the canyon. The bright toned part at the bottom of the image is the northern extent of Beatis Mensa, a large layered deposit within the canyon.
