# Mars Rover Team Celebrates Their Perseverance * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA23xxx/PIA23763_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA23763.jpg) (6046 x 4543) (4.9 MB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA23763.tif) (6046 x 4543) (68.3 MB)

Caption:

Team members of NASA's next Mars mission applaud as mission manager Jennifer Trosper unveils a nameplate with "Perseverance" laser-etched into its surface. The image was taken at the agency's Jet Propulsion Laboratory on March 5, 2020.
