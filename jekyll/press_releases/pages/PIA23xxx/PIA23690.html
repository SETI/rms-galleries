# Kepler Planet 1649c Surface View Artist's Concept * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA23xxx/PIA23690_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA23690.jpg) (3840 x 2160) (315.6 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA23690.tif) (3840 x 2160) (7.7 MB)

Caption:

This artist's concept shows what exoplanet Kepler-1649c could look like on its surface. The planet is the closest to Earth in size and temperature found yet in data from the Kepler space telescope.
