# Mineral Map Created During a Test of SHERLOC * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA23xxx/PIA23895_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA23895.jpg) (3214 x 2411) (247.3 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA23895.tif) (3214 x 2411) (4.4 MB)

Caption:

In this test image by SHERLOC (Scanning Habitable Environments with Raman & Luminescence for Organics & Chemicals), an instrument aboard NASA's Perseverance rover, each color represents a different mineral detected on a rock's surface.

Background Info:

For more information about the mission, go to https://mars.nasa.gov/mars2020/ .
