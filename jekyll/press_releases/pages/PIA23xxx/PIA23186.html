# Cavi Angusti * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA23xxx/PIA23186_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA23186.jpg) (992 x 2633) (226.0 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA23186.tif) (992 x 2633) (2.3 MB)

Caption:


Context image

Cavi Angusti is located near the south polar cap and consists of large irregular steep-sided depressions termed cavi. Layering is visible within the large cavi at the top of the image.

Orbit Number: 76085 Latitude: -81.7438 Longitude: 286.943 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2019-02-08 02:43
