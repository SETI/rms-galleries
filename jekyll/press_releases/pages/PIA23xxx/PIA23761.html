# NASA Rover Naming Contest Winner Alexander Mather * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA23xxx/PIA23761_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA23761.jpg) (3648 x 5472) (1.3 MB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA23761.tif) (3648 x 5472) (37.2 MB)

Caption:

Seventh-grader Alexander Mather of Lake Braddock Secondary School in Burke, Virginia, submitted the winning entry in the agency's " Name the Rover " essay contest, making the case to name the Mars 2020 rover "Perseverance." For the contest, NASA received 28,000 essays (150 words or less) from K-12 students from every U.S. state and territory.

Background Info:

For more information about the mission, go to https://mars.nasa.gov/mars2020/ .
