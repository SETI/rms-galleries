# Daedalia Planum * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA23xxx/PIA23085_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA23085.jpg) (672 x 1411) (154.4 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA23085.tif) (672 x 1411) (749.9 kB)

Caption:


Context image

This VIS image shows a small portion of Daedalia Planum. The lava flows in this image originated at Arsia Mons. Individual flows have lengths up to almost 180km (~111 miles). The longest Hawaiian lava flow is only 51km (~31 miles). The area of Daedalia Planum is 2.9 million square km - more than 4 times the size of Texas.
