# Micoud Crater * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA23xxx/PIA23538_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA23538.jpg) (1457 x 2666) (243.6 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA23538.tif) (1457 x 2666) (2.4 MB)

Caption:


Context image

Today's VIS image shows the southeastern portion of Micoud Crater. Micoud Crater is located in Vastitas Borealis. A small group of sand dunes is located on the floor of the crater, visible in the upper left portion of the image.
