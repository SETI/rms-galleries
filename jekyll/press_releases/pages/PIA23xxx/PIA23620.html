# SuperCam's Mast Unit Being Tested at Los Alamos National Laboratory * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA23xxx/PIA23620_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA23620.jpg) (4982 x 3349) (1.6 MB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA23620.tif) (4982 x 3349) (50.1 MB)

Caption:

The Mast Unit for Mars 2020's SuperCam, shown being tested here, will use a laser to vaporize and study rock material on the Red Planet's surface.

Background Info:

JPL is building and will manage operations of the Mars 2020 rover for the NASA Science Mission Directorate at the agency's headquarters in Washington.

For more information about the mission, go to https://mars.nasa.gov/mars2020/ .
