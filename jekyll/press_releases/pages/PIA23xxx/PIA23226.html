# Polar Margin - False Color * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA23xxx/PIA23226_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA23226.jpg) (594 x 1449) (76.5 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA23226.tif) (594 x 1449) (2.1 MB)

Caption:


Context image

Today's false color image shows part of the margin of the north polar cap and the surrounding plains. This image was collected during the northern summer season.

The THEMIS VIS camera contains 5 filters. The data from different filters can be combined in multiple ways to create a false color image. These false color images may reveal subtle variations of the surface not easily identified in a single band image.
