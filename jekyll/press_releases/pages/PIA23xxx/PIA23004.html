# K2-288Bb (Artist's Illustration) * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA23xxx/PIA23004_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA23004.jpg) (6000 x 3375) (1.2 MB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA23004.tif) (6000 x 3375) (33.5 MB)

Caption:

The newfound planet K2-288Bb, illustrated here, is slightly smaller than Neptune. Located about 226 light-years away, it orbits the fainter member of a pair of cool M-type stars every 31.3 days.
