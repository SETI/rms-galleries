# Shallow Lightning on Jupiter (Illustration) * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA23xxx/PIA23983_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA23983.jpg) (2539 x 3373) (405.9 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA23983.tif) (2539 x 3373) (20.1 MB)

Caption:

This illustration uses data obtained by NASA's Juno mission to depict high-altitude electrical storms on Jupiter. Juno's sensitive Stellar Reference Unit camera detected unusual lightning flashes on Jupiter's dark side during the spacecraft's close flybys of the planet.

Background Info:

More information about Juno is at http://www.nasa.gov/juno and http://missionjuno.swri.edu .
