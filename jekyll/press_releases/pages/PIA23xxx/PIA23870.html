# Measuring the Masses and Diameters of the TRAPPIST-1 Planets * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA23xxx/PIA23870_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA23870.jpg) (3840 x 2160) (417.9 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA23870.tif) (3840 x 2160) (9.8 MB)

Caption:

Measuring the mass and diameter of a planet reveals its density, which can give scientists clues about its composition. Scientists now know the density of the seven TRAPPIST-1 planets with a higher precision than any other planets in the universe, other than those in our own solar system.
