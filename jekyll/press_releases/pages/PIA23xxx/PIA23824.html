# Mars Helicopter and Perseverance Rover * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA23xxx/PIA23824_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA23824.jpg) (4451 x 2504) (1.7 MB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA23824.tif) (4451 x 2504) (30.9 MB)

Caption:

The Mars Helicopter, visible in lower center of the image, was attached to the belly of NASA's Perseverance rover at Kennedy Space Center on April 6, 2020. The helicopter will be deployed onto the Martian surface about two-and-a-half months after Perseverance lands.

Background Info:

For more information about the mission, go to https://mars.nasa.gov/technology/helicopter/ .
