# Chasma Australe * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA23xxx/PIA23256_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA23256.jpg) (499 x 5388) (429.3 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA23256.tif) (499 x 5388) (2.7 MB)

Caption:


Context image

Today's VIS image shows part of the South Pole. Chasma Australe crosses diagonally through the middle of the image. The layers that comprise the polar cap are visible along the sides of the chasma.

Orbit Number: 76217 Latitude: -85.2706 Longitude: 96.9588 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2019-02-18 23:34
