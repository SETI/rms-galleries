# Cleaning Mars 2020 * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA23xxx/PIA23717_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA23717.jpg) (1600 x 1067) (182.1 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA23717.tif) (1600 x 1067) (4.0 MB)

Caption:

Wiping down hardware is part of the strategy to limit the number of Earth microbes going to the Red Planet for NASA's Mars 2020 Perseverance mission. This cleaning takes place in the Spacecraft Assembly Facility clean room at NASA's Jet Propulsion Laboratory in Southern California.

Background Info:

For more information about the mission, go to https://mars.nasa.gov/mars2020/ .
