# Lobate Scarps on the Moon * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA23xxx/PIA23236_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA23236.jpg) (1631 x 1010) (267.7 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA23236.tif) (1631 x 1010) (1.6 MB)

Caption:

This image of lobate scarps - a kind of curved hill - was taken near a region of the Moon called Mare Frigoris by NASA's Lunar Reconnaissance Orbiter (LRO).

Background Info:

The LRO project is managed by NASA's Goddard Space Flight Center in Greenbelt, Maryland, on behalf of NASA's Science Mission Directorate.
