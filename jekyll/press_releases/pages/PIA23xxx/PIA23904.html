# Libya Montes Channel * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA23xxx/PIA23904_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA23904.jpg) (1359 x 2644) (271.8 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA23904.tif) (1359 x 2644) (2.5 MB)

Caption:


Context image

Today's VIS image shows a section of unnamed channel in Libya Montes. Libya Montes are south of Isidis Planitia, and are remnants of the crater rim created by the Isidis meteor impact.

Orbit Number: 80795 Latitude: 0.708331 Longitude: 89.3586 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2020-03-01 23:28
