# Margaritifer Terra * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA23xxx/PIA23577_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA23577.jpg) (681 x 1428) (122.8 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA23577.tif) (681 x 1428) (750.6 kB)

Caption:


Context image

A large enclosed basin is located in Margaritifer Terra. Several small channels flow into the basin and multiple large outflow channels cross it from south to north. This VIS image shows are region of graben within the center of the basin.

Orbit Number: 79152 Latitude: -18.905 Longitude: 331.607 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2019-10-18 16:32
