# Noachis Terra Crater * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA22xxx/PIA22844_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA22844.jpg) (1316 x 2674) (266.7 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA22844.tif) (1316 x 2674) (2.5 MB)

Caption:


Context image

This VIS image is located in an unnamed crater in Noachis Terra. The sinuous feature near the bottom of the crater rim appears to have been caused by down slope movement of materials. The sinuous nature may have been created by inclusion of a volatile material such as ice.
