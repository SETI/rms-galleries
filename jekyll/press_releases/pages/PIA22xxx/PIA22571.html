# InSight Lander with Solar Arrays Deployed * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA22xxx/PIA22571_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA22571.jpg) (3830 x 2148) (842.8 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA22571.tif) (3830 x 2148) (21.1 MB)

Caption:

This artist's illustration shows NASA's InSight lander on the surface of Mars, with its solar arrays deployed.

Background Info:

NASA's Jet Propulsion Laboratory, a division of Caltech in Pasadena, California, manages the InSight Project for NASA's Science Mission Directorate, Washington. Lockheed Martin Space, Denver, Colorado built the spacecraft. InSight is part of NASA's Discovery Program, which is managed by NASA's Marshall Space Flight Center in Huntsville, Alabama.

For more information about the mission, go to https://mars.nasa.gov/insight .
