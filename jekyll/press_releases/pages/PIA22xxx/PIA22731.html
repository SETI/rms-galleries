# Lyot Crater - False Color * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA22xxx/PIA22731_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA22731.jpg) (644 x 2703) (112.3 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA22731.tif) (644 x 2703) (2.4 MB)

Caption:


Context image

This VIS image shows part of Lyot Crater. Lyot Crater is a large complex crater located in the lowlands between Arabia Terra and Vastitas Borealis. A small region of sand dunes can be seen in the image.

The THEMIS VIS camera contains 5 filters. The data from different filters can be combined in multiple ways to create a false color image. These false color images may reveal subtle variations of the surface not easily identified in a single band image.
