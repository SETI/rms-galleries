# Mapping the Great Blue Spot * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA25xxx/PIA25036_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA25036.jpg) (4500 x 3500) (587.8 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA25036.tif) (4500 x 3500) (4.5 MB)

Caption:

This graphic shows a comparison of the observed change in Jupiter's radial magnetic field, over time, as well as the change calculated from the model, assuming eastward drift of the "Great Blue Spot" (GBS).
