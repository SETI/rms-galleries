# Earth Interior Graphic * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA25xxx/PIA25063_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA25063.jpg) (4500 x 3500) (327.1 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA25063.tif) (4500 x 3500) (2.0 MB)

Caption:

This model of Earth's interior includes a solid, mostly iron inner core beneath a convective liquid iron core that extends to half Earth's radius. Above that is a silicate mantle rich in iron and magnesium and a thin crust of lighter silicates.

Background Info:

More information about Juno is at https://www.nasa.gov/juno and https://missionjuno.swri.edu .
