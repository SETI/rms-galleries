# Jupiter's South Temperate Belt * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA24xxx/PIA24962_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA24962.jpg) (1176 x 2160) (157.8 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA24962.tif) (1176 x 2160) (4.7 MB)

Caption:

This JunoCam image of Jupiter's south temperate belt and Great Red Spot was taken on Dec. 30, 2020.

Wide enough to contain Earth, the Great Red Spot is the most dominant atmospheric feature in the planet's southern hemisphere.

Background Info:

More information about Juno is at https://www.nasa.gov/juno and https://missionjuno.swri.edu .
