# Ascraeus Mons * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA24xxx/PIA24141_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA24141.jpg) (605 x 2698) (102.5 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA24141.tif) (605 x 2698) (759.6 kB)

Caption:


Context image
Today's image shows the southwestern flank of Ascraeus Mons. Ascraeus is the northenmost of the three aligned Tharsis volcanoes and is the tallest at 18,225meters (59,793ft).

Orbit Number: 82112 Latitude: 9.73717 Longitude: 253.773 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2020-06-18 10:06
