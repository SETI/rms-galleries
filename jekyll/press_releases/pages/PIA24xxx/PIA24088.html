Caption:


Context image
This VIS image shows a section of Her Desher Vallis. This channel is located in Noachis Terra. Her Desher is the Egyptian word for Mars.

Orbit Number: 81898 Latitude: -25.2878 Longitude: 312.226 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2020-05-31 19:01
