# Cavi Angusti * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA24xxx/PIA24359_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA24359.jpg) (496 x 1404) (64.0 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA24359.tif) (496 x 1404) (668.1 kB)

Caption:


Context image
Cavi Angusti is located near the south polar cap and consists of large irregular steep-sided depressions termed cavi. This VIS image is located in the center of the region. Both layering and gullies are visible in the center of the image.
