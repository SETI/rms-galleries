# Sampling Mars 2020 Sample Tube 241 * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA24xxx/PIA24294_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA24294.jpg) (9504 x 6336) (4.0 MB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA24294.tif) (9504 x 6336) (123.9 MB)

Caption:

A technician working on the Mars 2020 Perseverance rover mission takes a sample from the surface of sample tube 241 to test for contamination. Each sample tube has its own unique serial number (seen on the gold-colored portion of the tube). The image was taken in a clean room facility at NASA's Jet Propulsion Laboratory in Southern California, where the tubes were developed and assembled.

Background Info:

JPL built and will manage operations of the Mars 2020 Perseverance rover for NASA.

For more information about the mission, go to: https://mars.nasa.gov/mars2020 .
