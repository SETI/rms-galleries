# Gale Crater * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA24xxx/PIA24951_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA24951.jpg) (692 x 1431) (68.9 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA24951.tif) (692 x 1431) (647.3 kB)

Caption:


Context image

Today's VIS image shows the large layered deposit on the floor of Gale. The Curiosity Rover is located in Gale Crater. Gale crater is 150km (90 miles) in diameter.

Orbit Number: 86309 Latitude: -4.67469 Longitude: 137.724 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2021-05-29 23:44
