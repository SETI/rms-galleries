# Mars 2020 Expanded Spacecraft Illustration * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA24xxx/PIA24128_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA24128.jpg) (3000 x 4500) (789.7 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA24128.tif) (3000 x 4500) (10.2 MB)

Caption:

This illustration depicts five major components of the Mars 2020 spacecraft. Top to bottom: cruise stage, backshell, descent stage, Perseverance rover and heat shield. The various components perform critical roles during the vehicle's cruise to Mars and its dramatic entry, descent, and landing.

Background Info:

For more information about the mission, go to https://mars.nasa.gov/mars2020/ .
