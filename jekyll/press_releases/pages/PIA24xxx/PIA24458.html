Caption:


Context image

The THEMIS VIS camera contains 5 filters. The data from different filters can be combined in multiple ways to create a false color image. These false color images may reveal subtle variations of the surface not easily identified in a single band image. Today's false color image shows part of the floor of Schroeter Crater. Schroeter Crater is located in Terra Sabaea and is 291 km (180 miles) in diameter.
