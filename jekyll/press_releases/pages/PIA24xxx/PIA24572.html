Figure 1

Weak but nearly continuous plasma oscillation events — visible as a thin red line in this graphic — connect stronger events in Voyager 1's Plasma Wave Subsystem data. The filtered data shows weaker signals while the alternate graph, with blue background, shows only the strong signals (Figure 1).
