Caption:


Context image
Today's VIS image shows part of Ross Crater. Located in Aonia Terra, the impact crater is 82 km (51 miles) in diameter. In addition to the dunes on the crater floor, the crater rim is dissected with numerous gullies, one of which is visible at top of the image.
