Caption:

Håvard Grip, chief pilot of NASA's Ingenuity Mars Helicopter, documents the details of each flight in the mission's logbook, The Nominal Pilot's Logbook for Planets and Moons , after each flight. Entries for Flights 9 and 10 are seen here.
