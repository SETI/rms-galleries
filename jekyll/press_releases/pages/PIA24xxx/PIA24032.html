# NASA Astronauts on Mars With Helicopter (Illustration) * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA24xxx/PIA24032_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA24032.jpg) (1920 x 1080) (220.0 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA24032.tif) (1920 x 1080) (4.6 MB)

Caption:

This illustration shows NASA astronauts working on the surface of Mars. A helicopter similar to the Ingenuity Mars Helicopter is airborne at left. Ingenuity is being carried aboard the Perseverance rover; it will be deployed to the Martian surface in the weeks after landing to test whether future helicopters could accompany robotic and human missions.
