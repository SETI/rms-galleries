# Southern Dunes * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA24xxx/PIA24364_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA24364.jpg) (1107 x 2816) (179.0 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA24364.tif) (1107 x 2816) (2.4 MB)

Caption:


Context image
Today's VIS image shows a field on dunes on the floor of an unnamed crater in southern Terra Sirenum. The tracks of dust devils can be seen elsewhere on the crater floor, as well as on the crater rim.

Orbit Number: 84004 Latitude: -61.0073 Longitude: 216.778 Instrument: VIS Captured: 2020-11-21 04:14
