# Toro Crater Rim - False Color * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA24xxx/PIA24656_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA24656.jpg) (700 x 1433) (75.8 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA24656.tif) (700 x 1433) (2.0 MB)

Caption:


Context image

The THEMIS VIS camera contains 5 filters. The data from different filters can be combined in multiple ways to create a false color image. These false color images may reveal subtle variations of the surface not easily identified in a single band image. Today's false color image shows the southeastern rim of Toro Crater. Toro Crater is located in Syrtis Major Planum and is 41km (25miles) in diameter.
