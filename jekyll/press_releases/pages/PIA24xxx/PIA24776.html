# Huo Hsing Vallis * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA24xxx/PIA24776_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA24776.jpg) (617 x 2697) (102.8 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA24776.tif) (617 x 2697) (832.1 kB)

Caption:


Context image

This VIS image shows part of Huo Hsing Vallis (bottom of image). Huo Hsing Vallis arose in Terra Sabaea, and flowed down from the highlands into the lowland region of Nilosyrtis Mensa (top of image).
