# Going Downhill * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA24xxx/PIA24112_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA24112.jpg) (679 x 1426) (79.0 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA24112.tif) (679 x 1426) (693.7 kB)

Caption:


Context image
This VIS image shows several channels located in Terra Sabaea. The flow was from the top of the image towards the bottom. These channels are located north of Hellas Planum and are following the topographic gradient down into the Hellas basin. None of these channels have been named.
