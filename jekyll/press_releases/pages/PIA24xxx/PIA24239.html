# Jupiter North Pole Detail * Click the [image above](/press_releases/medium/PIA24xxx/PIA24239_med.jpg) for a larger view * [Full-Res JPEG](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/jpeg/PIA24239.jpg) (809 x 1162) (162.4 kB) * [Full-Res TIFF](https://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/tiff/PIA24239.tif) (809 x 1162) (2.8 MB)

Caption:

This image of Jupiter's norther polar region was processed by citizen scientist Emma Wälimäki. The data for the image was collected during Juno's 29th perijove (PJ) pass on Sept. 16, 2020.

Background Info:

JunoCam's raw images are available for the public to peruse and process into image products at
https://missionjuno.swri.edu/junocam/processing . More information about NASA citizen science can be found at https://science.nasa.gov/citizenscience and https://www.nasa.gov/solve/opportunities/citizenscience .

More information about Juno is at https://www.nasa.gov/juno and https://missionjuno.swri.edu .
